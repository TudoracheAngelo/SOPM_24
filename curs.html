Our Courses

At NetACAD, we offer a diverse range of courses to meet your networking needs, whether you're just starting out or looking to advance in your career. Our curriculum is designed with a blend of theory and hands-on practice, ensuring you gain real-world skills that employers are looking for. Explore our most popular offerings: Beginner Courses Networking Fundamentals: Understand the basics of networking, IP addressing, and LAN/WAN concepts. Introduction to Cybersecurity: Learn the core principles of keeping networks secure from threats. CompTIA Network+ Prep: Build a solid foundation in networking and prepare for the globally recognized CompTIA Network+ certification. Intermediate Courses Cisco CCNA Certification: Master routing, switching, and network protocols to earn your Cisco CCNA certification. Wireless Networking: Explore wireless technologies and design secure, scalable wireless networks. Cloud Networking Essentials: Learn to deploy and manage network services in cloud environments like AWS and Azure. Advanced Courses Network Security & Ethical Hacking: Dive deep into network security practices and prepare for certifications like CompTIA Security+ and CEH. Advanced Routing & Switching: Delve into complex networking concepts and configurations. Automation & Network Programming: Learn to automate network tasks with Python and other scripting tools to streamline operations. Each course is available in multiple formats, including live online sessions, self-paced learning, and in-person classes. Check out our course catalog to find the right fit for you!
